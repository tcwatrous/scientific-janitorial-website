RCustomizing a daily checklist for your culinary operation that aligns with your values

We create a daily checklist and cleaning sched-ule for commercial kitchens that is essential in order to ensure the safety and hygiene of your facility. This checklist includes:

  • Wiping down surfaces
  • Sweeping & mopping floors
  • cleaning floor drains
  • disposing of garbage properly
  • backsplashes
  • Vents
  • Kitchen exhaust filters
  • And more

Our technicians send reports of malfunctioning equipment or unsafe conditions to address. By having a daily cleaning checklist in place, you can ensure that your kitchen is always up to safe standards of hygiene and sanitation which ensures our mutual success.
